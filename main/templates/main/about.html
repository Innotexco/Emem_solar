About Emem Energy

Empowering Nigeria with Sustainable Energy

Emem Energy Innovation Ltd is Nigeria's leading manufacturer of high-quality lithium battery energy storage systems, crafted locally to deliver reliability, affordability, and sustainability.

Our Journey

Building Nigeria's Energy Future

Founded in Nigeria, Emem Energy Innovation Ltd emerged from a vision to bridge the gap in reliable energy access. We saw the challenges faced by homes and businesses due to inconsistent power supply and high costs of imported solutions.

By establishing state-of-the-art local manufacturing, we've created energy storage systems that meet global standards while being tailored to Nigeria's unique needs. Our commitment to innovation and quality has made us a trusted name nationwide.

With thousands of systems powering Nigeria, we're not just a manufacturer—we're a partner in building a sustainable energy future.
