FAQ

Frequently
Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most from customers across Nigeria.

How long does it take to get a quote?

We typically respond to quote requests within 24 hours. For urgent requests, call our hotline and speak directly with a sales representative for immediate pricing.

Do you offer installation services?

We sell battery systems only, but we work with a network of certified installers across Nigeria. We can connect you with trusted professionals in your area.

What is your warranty coverage?

All our energy storage systems come with a comprehensive 7-year warranty covering manufacturing defects and performance issues. Extended options are also available.

How do I find a distributor near me?

Contact us with your location and we'll connect you with the nearest authorised distributor. We have partners across all major cities in Nigeria.

Can I purchase directly from you?

Yes — we sell both directly to consumers and through our distributor network. Contact us to discuss the best purchasing option for your location and needs.

Which inverters are compatible?

Our lithium battery systems are compatible with all major solar inverter brands. Our technical team can advise on the best configuration for your existing setup.
