How long does it take to get a quote?

We typically respond to quote requests within 24 hours. For urgent requests, you can call our hotline and speak directly with a sales representative who can provide immediate pricing information.

Do you offer installation services?

We sell battery systems only, but we work with a network of certified installers across Nigeria. We can connect you with trusted installation professionals in your area.

What is your warranty coverage?

All our energy storage systems come with a comprehensive 5-year warranty covering manufacturing defects and performance issues. Extended warranty options are also available.

How do I find a distributor near me?

Contact us via phone or email with your location, and we'll connect you with the nearest authorized distributor. We have distribution partners across all major cities in Nigeria.

Can I purchase directly from you?

Yes! We sell both directly to consumers and through our distributor network. Contact us to discuss the best purchasing option for your specific needs and location.
